About

My name is Michael, and this is my website. I teach music theory at Furman University in Greenville, SC. I’m into a lot of things: music theory, computer programming, jazz, hacker culture, text editing, hip culture, transformational theory, Schenkerian theory and analysis, math, and learning new things. You can read about some of the things I’ve done on the projects page.

About this site

This site, www.mcclimon.org, is hosted on Github Pages. It’s now a static site generated with jekyll, after former stints in Ruby, Perl, and PHP. The serifed body font on this website is the open-source Junicode. The sans-serif in the header is PT Sans Narrow, from the folks over at Paratype. PT Sans is hosted on Google web fonts, and Junicode is available on their website.
