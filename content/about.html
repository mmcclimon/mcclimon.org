About

My name is Michael (he/him), and this is my website. I live in Philadelphia, and I work at Fastmail. I’m into a lot of things: music theory, computer programming, jazz, hacker culture, text editing, hip culture, transformational theory, Schenkerian theory and analysis, math, and learning new things. You can read about some of the things I’ve done on the projects page.
